About Us

Sports Medicine is a field of medical practice typically based around athletic injuries. A sports med physician primarily focuses on sport related injuries such as broken bones, conditioning, physical therapy, rehabilitation, joint, bone, muscle health and lots more. Sports medicine physicians will also often treat athletes with back pain due to strain and injury placed on the back from various sports. Sports medicine doctors are medical professionals who focus on the prevention, diagnosis and treatment of injuries related to athletic activities. Sports medicine prepares the physicians for work in both traditional hospital and clinic settings, and also going to sporting events at both the professional and college levels. 

  • Sprains and strains
  • Dislocations
  • Ligament injuries
  • Fractures
  • Apophysitis
  • Tendinitis
  • Overuse injuries
  • Cartilage injuries
  • Exercise-induced asthma
  • Concussions
  • Nutrition and supplement issues
  • Diabetes
  • Stress fractures
